\textbf{``I Did It!''} \\
October 24, 2023 \\
Kalie Jia
\end{center}

My eyes hurt. My lips cried out for water. I stared at the white cement sidewalk, grains of sand shining like mirrors.

I looked up and saw the pendulum ride swinging like a hammer against the sun. My heart sank. Amusement park rides were not for me.

Despite my protests, my uncle bought tickets. I watched my siblings ride while doubt twisted in my stomach.

Eventually, I joined them on a small dinosaur ride. The joy returned. Then came the water ride. I swallowed my fear and climbed aboard.

I plunged down, drenched, laughing. The thrill erased my nerves.

Later, I stood beneath the pendulum ride again. This time, I climbed in.

Music roared. Air blasted my face. I screamed—and survived.

When the ride ended, disbelief and pride filled my mind.

I did it.
